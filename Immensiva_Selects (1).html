The Nights

Every evening of the festival, the gallery becomes a cinema — and the cinema has no screen.

Immensiva Selects is a curated programme of virtual-reality films, screened nightly across IMMENSIVA Festival 2026. Audiences don't watch from a distance — they enter each work, surrounded on every side.

The programme brings together two worlds: new voices discovered through an international open call, and standout pieces drawn from the celebrated Unframed Collection — from first-time makers to established names, screening across the festival's evenings.

It runs alongside the residency exhibition as a complementary event — a second way into the festival, and the first chapter of Espronceda's ongoing programme of curated XR and immersive experiences.

Why a cinema, every night

The immersive installations take you in one at a time. A nightly cinema takes everyone — seated, scheduled, and surrounded — turning the wait into the main event.

Who can apply

  • Open internationally to individual artists, studios, and collectives.
  • VR, 360°, and volumetric works — runtime 5–25 minutes preferred.
  • Completed works only. No works-in-progress or concepts.
  • Previously screened work is welcome, provided rights are clear for the Barcelona screening.

What to send

  • The completed work in a festival-compatible playback format.
  • A synopsis (max 200 words) and short artist statement (max 150 words).
  • Technical specs: engine, headset compatibility, runtime, interaction model.
  • Artist or studio bio, exhibition history, and a still or trailer.
